How To Buy shibi

Set Up Your Wallet

Setup your wallet using metamask or trustwallet to get started. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to metamask.io.

Get Some ETH

Have ETH in your wallet to switch to SHiBi. If you don’t have any ETH, you can buy directly on metamask, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

Buy & Swap For SHibi

Connect to pancakeswap. Connect your wallet. Paste the SHibi token address into Pancakeswap or poocoin , select Shibi, and confirm. When Metamask prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.

ENJOY

After the above step there is no need for more work, JUST HOLD and ENJOY the ride to the moon.
